About Us






The business started when a group of individual student of FEU Institute of Technology met in an event near Lacson called, "The Troika". The students became friends because of their common interest, which are programming and eating corn. The students decided to create a physical restaurant that offers good quality and tastes of a different food with corn recipes for catering. As the days goes by the number of clients are getting high so the students wanted their catering restaurant to be known in other places and decided to develop a website for an easy transaction with their clients.
